```{prf:theorem} Projection Theorem
:label: thm-projection

Let $C \subset \mathcal{H}$ be nonempty, closed, and convex. For every
$x \in \mathcal{H}$, there exists a unique $y \in C$ minimizing $\|x-y\|$.
```
